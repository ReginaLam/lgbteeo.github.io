LGBT EEO

LGBT Equal Employment Opportunity:
The tech world is getting pretty good about LGB rights. Let's get better about trans rights. And make everyone who needs tech improve. Add your name and show your support.

About this

Do people frequently ask you for help promoting a tech job? Or anything? Let's require some civil rights from the employers and events we help promote.

Sign your name below and commit to asking employers if they allow LGBT discrimination before agreeing to help them promote anything.

Here's what to do

If someone asks you to help promote a job opening, event, or anything, you commit to ask...

"Hey, before I promote your thing, I need to know:

  1. Does your company include LGBT employees in its EEO nondiscrimination policy?
  2. Does your company offer benefits equally to LGBT employees, their partners, and their dependents? Including comprehensive medical care for Trans employees?

Thank you!"

Or however you want to phrase it. :) Caveat: I work for the fed gov, and we don't offer comprehensive medical care for Trans employees yet, unfortunately. Not everyone will be there yet. But it's important to ask.
